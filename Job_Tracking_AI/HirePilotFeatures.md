# 🚀 HirePilot: Your Job Search Command Center

**HirePilot** is a clean, intelligent, and focused job search management application. It’s designed to help you stop worrying about spreadsheets and start taking action on your next role.

---

## 🧭 Core Navigation Features

### 1. My Day (The Dashboard)
*   **What it is**: Your daily mission control.
*   **How to use it**: Check this every morning! It shows your current application streak, total job count, and any urgent alerts.
*   **✨ Daily Fuel**: A permanent motivation widget at the top of the sidebar to keep your spirits high during the grind.

### 2. My Jobs (The Pipeline)
*   **What it is**: A visual "Kanban" board of your entire career pipeline.
*   **How to use it**: Use columns like **Wishlist**, **Applied**, **Interviewing**, **Offer**, and **Rejected**.
*   **Drag & Drop**: Simply click and move a card from one column to another as you progress through interviews.
*   **Search & Filter**: Quickly find any job by typing the company name or filtering by priority (High/Medium/Low).
*   **Focus Mode**: Toggle this "On" to instantly hide everything except your most urgent tasks and high-priority interviews.

### 3. Next Steps (Action Engine)
*   **What it is**: Your automated to-do list.
*   **How to use it**: HirePilot automatically scans your data and shows you:
    *   📅 **Overdue Follow-ups**: Jobs where your follow-up date has passed.
    *   ⏰ **Stale Jobs**: Jobs you haven't touched or updated in over 7 days.
    *   🔥 **Urgent Wishlist**: High-priority roles you haven't applied to yet.

### 4. My Stats (Analytics)
*   **What it is**: A data-driven look at your progress.
*   **How to use it**: See your "Applied vs. Interview" success rate and track your volume over time to make sure you're staying consistent.

### 5. Settings (Privacy & Data)
*   **What it is**: Your data management hub.
*   **Privacy First**: No data ever leaves your browser. It’s all stored locally on your device.
*   **Export/Import**: Create a JSON backup file to keep your data safe or move it to another computer.

---

## 🃏 Job Card Features
Every job card is a powerful mini-hub. Hover over a card to see your options:
*   **✏️ Edit**: Open a dedicated modal to update role details, set follow-up dates, or add interview notes/checklists.
*   **🗑️ Delete**: Remove a job permanently from your tracker.
*   **🔗 View Job**: Click the link icon to instantly jump to the original job posting.
*   **📅 Date Tags**: See exactly how long ago you applied and when your next action is due.

---

## ⚡ Pro Tips for HirePilot
1.  **Use the Notes field**: When editing a card, use the notes area to jot down salary expectations or common interviewer names before you hop on a call.
2.  **Toggle Focus Mode**: Use this to block out the noise when you only have 10 minutes and need to see exactly what warrants your attention *right now*.
3.  **Regular Backups**: Since HirePilot respects your privacy and stores data locally, remember to use the **Settings** page to download a backup file once a week.
